Privacy Policy for RexSound

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

At RexSound, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

We collect and store only your email address when you sign in to the app. We do not collect any other personal data, usage behavior, or device identifiers.

Purpose of Data Collection

Your email address is used for the following purposes:

  • Authenticate your login and manage your account
  • Save and sync your preferences across devices
  • Send important updates or optional marketing communications (if you consent)

How We Handle Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third-party advertisers. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs that access personal data. Your data is securely stored and used only to operate essential app features.

Consent and Control

By signing in, you consent to the use of your email for the purposes stated above. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account anytime through the "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings menu. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our system unless we are legally required to retain it.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct or update your email address
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account and data
